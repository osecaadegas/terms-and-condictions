Affiliate Program Terms & Conditions

Last updated: May 19, 2025

1. Allowed Promotion Methods

Affiliates may promote our gambling sites through approved channels such as:

  • Websites and blogs
  • Social media platforms
  • Email marketing

All promotional activities must comply with these terms and must not mislead users or violate any applicable laws or regulations.

2. Content Restrictions

Promotional materials must not include any of the following:

  • Illegal or harmful content
  • Copyrighted material (without permission)
  • Adult, violent, or offensive content
  • Defamatory or misleading statements

Affiliates must ensure all content is appropriate and aligns with responsible gaming standards.

3. Tracking & Reporting

Affiliates will use unique tracking links to identify referred users. Performance reports, including registrations and revenue data, will be provided by the gambling operator to track affiliate success.

4. Commission Calculation

Commissions are typically based on the net revenue generated by referred users. Payment terms, including the frequency and method of payout, will be clearly outlined in the affiliate agreement.

5. Affiliate Responsibilities

Affiliates must:

  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Promote responsible gambling practices
  • Avoid targeting minors or vulnerable individuals

6. Gambling Operator Responsibilities

Operators are responsible for:

  • Ensuring fair and transparent gambling services
  • Protecting minors and vulnerable groups
  • Providing accurate performance data and timely commission payments

7. Termination

Either party may terminate the agreement under specific conditions, including:

  • Breach of these terms
  • Failure to meet agreed performance targets
  • Legal or regulatory changes affecting the partnership

8. Data Privacy

Affiliates must comply with all data protection laws, including GDPR, when handling user data. This includes:

  • Lawful collection and processing of data
  • Maintaining data security and confidentiality

9. Confidentiality

Affiliates must keep sensitive information confidential, including:

  • Commission structures
  • Payment terms
  • Any other proprietary or business-related information
